Features

Performance

Unmatched power and precision. Experience acceleration like never before. The highest-performing units from BMW typically feature six cylinders; however, much of the history and legacy of the company rests on its 4-cylinder engine. The E30 M3’s high-revving engine, balanced chassis, and precise handling made it a standout in its time and a beloved model among enthusiasts. Its performance capabilities, combined with its racing pedigree, have solidified its place as a classic in BMW's M lineup. Even today, it’s regarded as one of the quintessential examples of a sports car with a perfect blend of performance and driver engagement.

Luxury

Interior designed with the finest materials and state-of-the-art technology. BMW is renowned for blending performance with luxury, and an interesting fact about their luxury offerings is the introduction of the BMW 7 Series’ "Executive Lounge" package. This package, available in models like the 750i and 760i, transforms the rear cabin into a first-class experience.

Innovation

Advanced features that redefine the driving experience. Safety and convenience at its best. BMW has consistently pushed the envelope in both driving experience and safety innovations. One fascinating example is their development of the "Active Driving Assistant" suite of technologies. This suite includes features like Adaptive Cruise Control, Lane Departure Warning, and Active Blind Spot Detection, all of which contribute to an enhanced driving experience and increased safety.
